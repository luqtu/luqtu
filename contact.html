What types of products do you trade?

We trade a wide range of products including daily necessities, apparel, electronics, hardware and building materials, and agricultural products. Our comprehensive product portfolio allows us to serve diverse market needs.

How can I become a partner?

To become a partner, please contact us through our business email (lisuosuo@luqtu.com) or fill out the contact form above. Our team will review your inquiry and get back to you within 2 business days.

Where is your mobile app available?

Our mobile application is available for download on both Google Play Store (Android) and Apple App Store (iOS). Search for "Luqiongtu" to find our official application.

What countries do you serve?

We serve customers in over 50 countries worldwide, with strong presence in Europe, North America, Asia, and other key markets. Contact us to confirm if we serve your region.
