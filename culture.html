Our Corporate Culture

Building excellence through values, vision, and innovation

Our Vision

Connecting Global Markets with Trust

At Taiyuan Luqiongtu Trading Co., Ltd., our vision is to be the most trusted bridge between Chinese manufacturers and global markets. We believe in creating lasting partnerships built on transparency, reliability, and mutual growth.

Through innovation and excellence, we strive to transform international trade, making it accessible, efficient, and beneficial for all stakeholders.

Our Mission

Empowering Business Growth Worldwide

Our mission is to provide comprehensive trade solutions that enable businesses to thrive in the global marketplace. We are committed to delivering exceptional value through quality products, efficient services, and innovative technology.

By leveraging our expertise in international trade and mobile technology, we help our partners expand their reach and achieve sustainable growth.

Our Journey
